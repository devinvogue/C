VISION


Our vision is a world of ending children suffering and poverty

MISSION


Our mission is to ensure that poor rural children are relieved against vulnerability to eradicate poverty and iniquity.

OBJECTIVES


A.

To promote & undertake development activities that will enable the needy, marginalised, vulnerable women and children and weaker section(s) in societies and to provide relief & perform works to clarity for those in need.

B.

To undertake programmes to protest against the evil practices of child labour, child abuse and women torture.

C.

To affiliate or work with other organizations for the same course or to enter any agreement with other trusts, societies and institution that may help to promote the objectives of the organization and that of other international bodies.

D.

Create awareness and impart health education regarding different disease prevalent in our society and promote all those efforts needed for prevention, cure and research for further findings.

E.

To provide need based training programmed to the poor rural youths, women, children and handicapped for self sufficiency & employment.

F.

To advocate and engage promoting activities spreading educational awareness among community such as primary, adult & non-formal education in rural areas.

G.

Working toward economic and livelihood sustainability, and build social equity and have a positive community impact; and sustain or improve environmental conditions.

H.

Application of integrated community development approaches as the core tool of operations.
